1. Valid Request (Happy Flow)
curl -X POST http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/v1/mobile-calls \
-H "Content-Type: application/json" \
-d '{
    "db_no": "DB1001",
    "campaign_id": "CMP001",
    "call_date": "2026-05-26",
    "start_epoch": 1779789000,
    "end_epoch": 1779789300,
    "user": "john.doe",
    "status_name": "Completed"
}'
